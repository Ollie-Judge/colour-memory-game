Colour Pattern Memory Game

Click anywhere on screen to start the game

Instructions:

Step 1:

Start the game by pressing any keyboard button and wait for the colour to flash, click the colour that flashed.

Step 2:

If the colour you clicked is correct, you should be informed youve moved onto level 2 and a new colour will flash.

Step 3:

You now need to remember the previous colours that have flashed, so for example, if in level one pink flashes and youve clicked that and have moved onto level 2 and then blue flashes, you need to press pink and then blue.

Step 4:

Repeat the process..... for example; if level 1 is yellow, press yellow, when level 2 arrives and shows blue for example, press yellow and blue, when level three comes and shows blue again press yellow, blue, blue for example.

Step 5:

Have fun and try to get to as high of a level as you can.
